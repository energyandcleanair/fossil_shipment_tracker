About account management

An issue has just been identified on the special caracters used to define your password. Some of them are no recognised and can lead, when trying to connect on Equasis, to an unknown password issue.
To avoid it, waiting a correction of Equasis, you can use on the following special caracters: ? ( , ) . : ! / % £ $ [ ] # § * \ - (the list will be amended as investigations progress).
